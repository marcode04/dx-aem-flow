# Optional Micro-Budget Boost Strategy (€50-100/month)

**Rule #1:** Never spend money on a post that flopped. Only amplify what's already working organically.

**Rule #2:** You don't NEED to spend anything. This entire plan works at €0. This file is for when you want to pour a little fuel on an existing fire.

---

## When to Boost (Decision Tree)

```
Did a post get unusually high organic engagement?
├── No → Don't spend anything. Write more content instead.
└── Yes → Is it on LinkedIn or Twitter?
    ├── LinkedIn post with 50+ likes / 10+ comments → Boost for €50-70
    ├── Twitter thread with 50+ retweets → Promote for €30-50
    └── Dev.to article trending → No boost needed, it's free traffic
```

---

## LinkedIn Post Boost (Best ROI for €50-100)

LinkedIn lets you boost personal posts directly from the post itself (no ad manager needed).

### How
1. Post goes live organically
2. Wait 24-48 hours — see if it gets traction
3. If it does (50+ likes, 10+ comments), click "Boost" on the post
4. Set budget: €50-70
5. Duration: 7 days
6. Targeting: Automatic (LinkedIn's algorithm already knows your audience) or narrow to Software Engineers + DevOps + Engineering Managers

### What to Expect
- €50 boost → ~3,000-8,000 additional impressions
- ~50-200 profile visits
- ~10-30 new followers
- Occasional inbound message from recruiter or fellow dev

### Best Posts to Boost
- "I built..." personal story posts (highest trust)
- Carousels showing the workflow (highest engagement)
- Hot takes about AI dev tools (highest shares)

## Twitter/X Post Promotion (€30-50)

### How
1. A thread gets 50+ retweets organically
2. Go to Twitter Ads → Promote tweet
3. Budget: €30-50
4. Target: Developer interests, followers of @AnthropicAI, @GitHubCopilot
5. Duration: 3-5 days

### What to Expect
- €30 promotion → ~5,000-15,000 additional impressions
- ~100-500 profile visits
- ~20-50 new followers

## Monthly Budget Allocation

| Month | Spend | What |
|-------|-------|------|
| Month 1 | €0 | Pure organic. Build content library. |
| Month 2 | €0 | Still organic. See what resonates. |
| Month 3 | €0-50 | If a post went viral, boost it. Otherwise save the money. |
| Month 4+ | €0-100 | Boost 1-2 best-performing posts per month. Only if earned. |

**Average real spend: probably €30-50/month.** Many months will be €0.

## Free Alternatives That Work Better Than Paid

| Instead of Paying For... | Do This for Free |
|--------------------------|-----------------|
| LinkedIn Ads | Post 3-5x/week from personal profile. Comment on 10 posts/day. |
| Google Ads | Write SEO-optimized articles on Dev.to/Hashnode (they rank fast). |
| Twitter Ads | Reply to big accounts. Quote-tweet with takes. Post threads. |
| Product Hunt Promoted | Build a supporter list of 50 people who will upvote on launch day. |

**The truth:** For a solo open-source developer, authentic personal content will ALWAYS outperform paid ads at this budget level. A genuine "I built this" LinkedIn post reaches more of the right people than a €100 ad campaign.
