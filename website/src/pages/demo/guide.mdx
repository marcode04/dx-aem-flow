<SectionHeading
  badge="Tips"
  badgeColor="success"
  title="Tips for Presenters"
  bg="primary"
/>
<Section>
  <HighlightBox severity="info" title="Making Your Demo Effective">
    <ul class="mt-2 space-y-1 text-sm">
      <li>Start with <code>/dx-ticket-analyze</code> on a ticket the audience knows well -- familiar context makes the output more impressive</li>
      <li>Show real ADO or Jira output (comments, PR reviews, wiki pages) not just the terminal -- that's what stakeholders see day-to-day</li>
      <li><strong>Business audience:</strong> focus on outcomes (time saved, quality improvements, automation coverage)</li>
      <li><strong>Developer audience:</strong> show architecture diagrams, config files, and how to extend the system</li>
      <li>Have ADO or Jira browser tabs pre-opened: Backlog, Pipelines, a PR with review comments, a work item with AI-generated comments</li>
      <li>Clean <code>.ai/specs/</code> directories before re-running demos to avoid stale output confusion</li>
    </ul>
  </HighlightBox>
</Section>
